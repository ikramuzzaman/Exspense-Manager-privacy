Privacy Policy – Expense Manager

Last updated: 01 March 2026

Expense Manager respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties.

Information Collection and Use

Expense Manager stores all data locally on your device only.

The data includes:

  • Income entries
  • Expense entries
  • Categories

No data is sent to any server or external service.

Third-Party Services

Expense Manager does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive permissions.

Data Security

All data remains stored locally on your device using Android system storage. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
